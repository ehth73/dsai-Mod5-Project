Payment and Transfer Policy

HSBC's payment and transfer policies are designed to provide a simple and efficient banking experience. Here are some key points regarding their policies:

PayNow: HSBC's PayNow service allows users to receive funds via electronic channels using their NRIC or mobile number, making it a convenient option for receiving funds without needing the bank account details of the sender. 

Transfer Fees and Charges: Users can choose to pay transfer fees and charges by themselves, share them with the beneficiary, or have them fully borne by the beneficiary. The fees and charges may vary depending on the intermediary or beneficiary banks involved in the transfer. 

Bill Payment: HSBC offers a range of bill payment options, including the ability to pay bills at over 1,000 locations islandwide and through the HSBC Singapore app. 

Transfers: HSBC provides various transfer options, including online banking, mobile banking, and ATMs, with specific fees and charges applicable to each method.
