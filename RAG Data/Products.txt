HSBC offers a wide range of personal, business, and investment products, including current accounts, loans, mortgages, credit cards, and structured investment solutions.
Personal Banking Products
HSBC provides several personal banking options to suit different needs:
Current Accounts: Standard accounts with digital banking, no monthly fees, and access to online management tools. 
HSBC Premier: A premium account offering wealth management, health and travel benefits, and rewards. Eligibility criteria apply. 
HSBC Advance: Designed for customers seeking enhanced banking services and support to manage finances and investments. 
Loans and Mortgages: Personal loans, home loans, and remortgaging options are available, with guidance for self-employed and foreign nationals. 
Credit Cards: Various cards with rewards, cashback, and travel benefits tailored to customer needs. 


Business Banking Products
HSBC supports businesses with a range of tailored solutions:
Business Accounts: Accounts with no monthly fees and digital banking support. 
Loans and Financing: Business loans, trade finance, and working capital solutions to support growth. 
Tailored Solutions: Customised products for hedging, portfolio diversification, and managing credit exposures. 

Investment and Structured Products
For sophisticated investors, HSBC offers structured products designed to meet specific risk-return objectives:
Structured Products: Customised investment solutions across asset classes including FX, equities, fixed income, and commodities. 
Bespoke Solutions: Products can be tailored in terms of wrappers, funding levels, minimum sizes, and currencies to optimize returns and manage risks.
Support and Expertise: HSBC provides in-depth structuring, pricing, and after-sales support through its global network. 
 
Additional Services
 Wealth Management: Investment advice, portfolio management, and financial planning.
 Insurance: Global insurance solutions for personal and business needs. 
 Digital Banking: Mobile and online platforms for account management, investments, and payments. 

HSBC product range is designed to cater to individuals, businesses, and institutional clients, providing flexibility, global access, and tailored financial solutions.
